About Us

Get to know us

Innovation Without Borders, Growth Without Limits

Founded in September 2024 in Cameroon and headquartered in Yaoundé-Cameroon, SAKURAFARI envisions becoming the premier gateway for any cutting-edge Japanese technologies into the Central African market, with Cameroon as the pivotal entry point. Our mission is to identify, adapt, and integrate innovative solutions from diverse sectors in Japan that promise transformative impact and sustainable growth for the region. At SAKURAFARI, we are committed to bridging the gap between Japan’s technological excellence and Central Africa’s growing potential, fostering a future of progress and collaboration.
