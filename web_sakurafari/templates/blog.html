TOKYO8 Organic Fertilizer Pilot Kicks off in Cameroon

Recognizing the untapped potential of sustainable agriculture in Cameroon, SAKURAFARI identified TOKYO8 to introduce its organic bio-fertilizer to local farming systems. Following enthusiastic reception during the 2024 Cameroon-Japan Business Fair, the interest quickly transitioned from conversation to implementation. Since December 2024, field trials have begun revealing the fertilizer’s powerful regenerative effects on soil health—positioning TOKYO8 as a game changer for the country’s agro-ecological transformation.

Portable Solar Power Stations Market Validation in Cameroon

In a nation where less than 1% of off-grid energy solutions are currently deployed, SAKURAFARI identified Solar World's portable power stations as a breakthrough opportunity for small and medium-sized enterprises grappling with energy insecurity. Acting swiftly, SAKURAFARI formalized a strategic MoU with Solar World and launched a field validation program in early 2025. The systems are now being tested across few shops in Douala in Cameroon—bringing clean, reliable power to the forefront of the local energy revolution.
